The dataset was collected to study bilingual language processing and semantic representation across languages. Full experimental details, task design, and analysis methods are described in the original publication:

Chen, C., Gong, X. L., Tseng, C., Klein, D. L., Gallant, J. L., & Deniz, F. (2026).  
Bilingual language processing relies on shared semantic representations that are modulated by each language.  
Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. 123 (9) e2503721123, https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2503721123
