The After-Work Cup celebrated its 10th anniversary at the TU Sportzentrum Waldschulallee. Numerous TU Berlin staff members competed in diverse, mixed teams across ten creative and engaging games, showcasing their skills, creativity, and team spirit.

Congratulations to Denizens Lab together with [NI group](https://www.tu.berlin/en/ni), who won the After-Work Cup!!
